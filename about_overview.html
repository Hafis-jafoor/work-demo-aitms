ABOUT US

AITMS is a business school that has changed the rules, so does our way of learning. While traditional colleges tend to offer a general curriculum, AITMS offers the opportunity for our students to specialize and further develop their expertise in Aviation, Logistics, IT & Commerce.

AITMS, is a brainchild of two young entrepreneur couples, Mr. Sreeraj M & Miss. Anju Sreeraj. Their vision to offer an alternative to conventional model of education, with a commitment to an innovative educational model capable of responding to the real needs of corporate business houses and professionals living in an era of constant change and transformation.

The Business School was born in 2015 and provide an integrated model, based on current trends and requirements in the sectors to train young professionals, who can become the backbone of various industries. Our goal is to train for different sectors demanded by the territories in which we live; able to undertake, innovate and create value for their businesses, their employees and society in a sustainable and profitable way.
