LEADERSHIP TEAM

Mr. Sreeraj M

Founder Chairman
AITMS Group of Institutions

A self made Young Entrepreneur with a vision to create employable workforce for this fast growing economy. He built this group from a small training center which he started in his home town, Mavelikkara. Today standing tall, AITMS has come a long way in the last eight years today with presence in 4 cities (Kochi, Kozhikode & Kannur) in Kerala and having offices in Bangalore & Mysore in Karnataka.

The Group has a Film Institute (Elements of Cinema https://elementsofcinema.in/), a Study Abroad Consultancy (BON Voyage - https://bonvoyage.education/) and a Career counselling Center. Thousands of Students entered their careers through these institutions and his vision toady is getting accomplished.
