AITMS - AERO INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY AND MANAGEMENT STUDIES is a leading aviation and logistics institute in South India, offering a wide range of courses in the field of aviation and logistics. AITMS is an authorized IATA training center, which ensures that students receive training of the highest quality, which is recognized globally. The institute offers different courses in aviation and logistics, which are designed to cater to the needs of students who wish to pursue a career in this field. These courses can be taken as stand-alone diploma programs or as part of the Integrated Degree Program (IDP), which provides students with the opportunity to earn a degree while studying at AITMS.

The courses offered at AITMS cover a wide range of topics related to ..

  • aviation and logistics
  • airline and airport operations
  • cargo handling
  • ground handling
  • customer service
  • air navigation and
  • air cargo management
The courses are designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of the industry and prepare them for a successful career in the aviation and logistics sector. AITMS has centers spread across South India, making it easy for students to access the institute's training facilities. The institute has state-of-the-art infrastructure and is equipped with modern classrooms, laboratories, and simulation facilities, which provide students with a hands-on learning experience.

Logistics

India’s logistics are estimated to account for about 14.4% of GDP. In 2019, the Indian logistics sector was valued at Rs. 15.1 lakh crore (US$ 190 billion).The development of the logistics sector is also reflected by the fact that India scored 90.3% in the United UNESCAP’s Global Survey on Digital and Sustainable Trade Facilitation conducted in 2021, which is an exceptional improvement from the score it secured in 2019 of 78.5%, brought about by gains in the scores of five important indicators. The score has shown a consistent improvement, with scores of 63.4% and 67.7% secured in 2015 and 2017, respectively.
